Solution A1:KarbonKredits

Incentivizing Low-Carbon Lifestyles

Solution: Implement a "KarbonKredit" system, where individuals and businesses earn tradable credits for reducing carbon footprints (e.g., using public transport, renewable energy). Credits can be exchanged for tax breaks, discounts, or services, creating a market-driven push for sustainability. Governments and NGOs oversee verification via blockchain for transparency. Impact: Encourages mass participation in emission reduction while making climate action financially rewarding.
